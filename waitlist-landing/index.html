FAQ

Honest answers.

Will this affect my credit score?

No. Krost is a soft-data layer — we read your gig platforms read-only and produce an independent score. No hard pulls, no credit-bureau footprint.

Which lenders accept the Krost Score today?

Three pilot lenders are live (announced at launch). We’re onboarding more weekly. Waitlist members get the partner list with their free report.

What does “on-chain attestation” actually mean for me?

Your score is published as a signed record on Base L2 — a low-fee Ethereum network. Any lender can verify the score is real and unaltered. You never expose raw earnings.

Is the free report really free?

Yes. One full report per waitlist signup, delivered by email. We monetize lender verifications, not gig workers.

How is my data stored?

Earnings sit encrypted in Supabase with row-level security. Only the score hash goes on-chain. You can wipe your data anytime.
