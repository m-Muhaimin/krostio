FAQ

Honest answers.

What is Krostio? Is it different from Krost?

Krostio is the expanded platform. What started as a single credit score is now four pillars: the Krost Score (alternative credit scoring), Krost Ledger (unified earnings history), Krost Verifier (lender-ready reports), and Krost Passport (on-chain financial identity). Same team, bigger vision — a complete financial identity for gig workers.

Will this affect my credit score?

No. Krostio is a soft-data layer — we read your gig platforms read-only and produce an independent income-based score. No hard pulls, no credit-bureau footprint. It doesn't affect FICO positively or negatively.

Which lenders accept the Krost Score today?

Three pilot lenders are live (announced at launch). We're onboarding more weekly. Waitlist members get the partner list with their free report. Lenders can verify any score on Base L2 in seconds.

Do I need a crypto wallet for the Passport?

No. Krostio uses Account Abstraction (ERC-4337) so we can create your wallet for you — gasless, email-based, no seed phrases needed. The Passport is optional and shows up automatically when your score reaches the Building tier (580+).

What does "on-chain attestation" mean for me?

Your score, ledger summary, and Passport data are published as signed records on Base L2 — a low-fee Ethereum network by Coinbase. Any lender can verify the data is real and unaltered. You never expose raw earnings. The record is permanent even if Krostio shuts down.

What's the free report — is it really free?

Yes. One full 12-page Income Verification Report per waitlist signup, delivered by email. Covers all four pillars: your score, ledger summary, a sample verifier report, and a Passport preview. We monetize lender verifications and Pro subscriptions, not gig workers.

How is my data stored?

Earnings sit encrypted in Supabase with row-level security. Only the score hash and Passport data go on-chain. You can export or wipe your data anytime from your settings page.
