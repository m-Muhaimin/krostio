Frequently asked

Common questions

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. We connect via Plaid — the same OAuth standard used by Chase, Venmo, and Robinhood. We never see or store your login credentials. You can disconnect anytime.

Can lenders or landlords trust this document?

The PDF is a self-generated statement sourced directly from your connected financial accounts. It includes a verification note explaining the data source. Many users have successfully used it for apartment and loan applications.

Which gig platforms does Krostio support?

Any platform that pays into a bank account Krostio can connect to via Plaid — including Uber, DoorDash, Lyft, Instacart, Upwork, Fiverr, TaskRabbit, Shipt, and hundreds more.

How far back does the income data go?

Krostio pulls up to 24 months of transaction history from your connected accounts. Free plan shows your current dashboard; Pro gives you full access to the complete history and PDF export.

Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?

Yes — cancel anytime, no questions asked. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period. We don't lock you in or penalize you for leaving.

What if I have fewer than 12 months of data?

Krostio will clearly flag the estimate as preliminary and note how many months of data it's based on. The annualized projection still works — it just shows "Preliminary estimate" on your statement.
