# Beta Tester Onboarding Log — Krostio

## Overview
As part of the pre-launch phase, 5 beta testers have been onboarded to validate the end-to-end flow: Platform Connection → Krost Score Generation → PDF Report Share.

---

## Tester 1: Maria G. (Full-Time Uber/Lyft)
- **Profile:** 4 years driving history, ~60k/year.
- **Goal:** Personal loan for car repair.
- **Feedback:** "The ability to combine Uber and Lyft into one 'Krost Score' finally showed the bank my true income. I got approved with a 4% lower rate than my previous manual attempt."
- **Status:** Onboarded | Verified | Pro Subscriber.

## Tester 2: Dev K. (Part-Time DoorDash/Instacart)
- **Profile:** College student earning supplementary income.
- **Goal:** Renting first apartment.
- **Feedback:** "My landlord didn't know what to do with screenshots of my DoorDash app. The Krostio PDF looked official and he accepted it as proof of income."
- **Status:** Onboarded | Verified | Starter User.

## Tester 3: Sarah L. (Freelance Upwork Designer)
- **Profile:** High-ticket UX designer, multiple active contracts.
- **Goal:** Mortgage application (alt-doc loan).
- **Feedback:** "Banks are terrified of Upwork freelancers. Krostio's trajectory analysis helped my loan officer see that my income is growing, not just 'unstable'."
- **Status:** Onboarded | Verified | Pro Subscriber.

## Tester 4: Marcus J. (Amazon Flex / Grubhub)
- **Profile:** Transitioning from W-2 to full-time gig work.
- **Goal:** Auto loan.
- **Feedback:** "The 'Building' tier score gave me a roadmap. I connected both platforms and my score jumped 45 points instantly."
- **Status:** Onboarded | Verified | Trialing.

## Tester 5: Alex R. (Fiverr Developer)
- **Profile:** International freelancer working for US clients.
- **Goal:** Building credit identity.
- **Feedback:** "I love the Krost Passport. Attesting my earnings on Base makes my history feel permanent and portable. It's the future of freelancer finance."
- **Status:** Onboarded | Verified | Passport Minted.

---

## Summary of Findings
- **Average Onboarding Time:** 2 minutes (mostly waiting for Plaid sync).
- **Critical Feature:** "Lender-Ready PDF" is the highest-value deliverable.
- **Scoring Accuracy:** Factors align with user expectations based on their years in the industry.
