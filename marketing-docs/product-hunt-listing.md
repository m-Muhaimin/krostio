## First Comment (Maker's Comment)

Hi Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Muhaimin, the founder of Krostio.

I built Krostio because 76 million Americans do gig work, but the traditional credit system still acts like they don't exist. If you've ever tried to rent an apartment or get a car loan as a full-time Uber driver or freelancer, you've probably heard: "We need two years of W-2s."

Krostio fixes this by creating a **translation layer** between gig platforms and lenders.

**What Krostio does:**
- 🔗 **Pillar 1: Ledger** — Connects to Uber, Lyft, Upwork, and 300+ others to create a unified earnings ledger.
- 📈 **Pillar 2: Krost Score** — Generates a 300-850 income verification metric based on stability and trajectory.
- 📄 **Pillar 3: Verifier** — Creates professional PDF reports and expiring links for lenders.
- 🆔 **Pillar 4: Passport** — Attests your score on-chain (Base L2) as a soul-bound financial identity.

Whether you're a gig worker tired of being ignored by banks, or a lender looking for better ways to verify 1099 income, I'd love to hear your feedback!

We're offering a **free PDF report** to the first 50 PH users who sign up today.

Muhaimin
