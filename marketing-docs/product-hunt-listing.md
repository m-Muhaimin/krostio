## Product Hunt Page Copy

### Headline
**Krostio — Your gig income is real. Krostio makes it count.**

### Subtitle
The credit score alternative for gig workers. Connect your Uber, DoorDash, Upwork, and other platforms to get a Krost Score (300-850) and share verified income reports with lenders, landlords, and anyone who needs proof of earnings. No W-2 required.

### Key Features (Bullet List)

- **Krost Score (300-850)** — The first credit score alternative built exclusively from gig income data across 9 factors including income volume, consistency, platform diversity, and tenure
- **Multi-platform aggregation** — Connect 8+ platforms (Uber, DoorDash, Lyft, Upwork, Instacart, Grubhub, Amazon Flex, Fiverr) to show your full earning picture in one place
- **Shareable Verifier links** — Generate a one-click income verification link and PDF report you can send to any lender, landlord, or leasing agent
- **Krost Passport on Base L2** — Optional on-chain soul-bound credential that cryptographically attests to your income data, portable across any platform or blockchain application
- **Lender-ready API** — REST API returning structured JSON income verification data with contextual scoring for financial institutions
- **Free to start** — Check your Krost Score at no cost. Shareable links and PDF reports available on paid tiers ($9 one-time or $19/mo)

### What Makes Krostio Different

Most income verification tools fall into one of two buckets: enterprise APIs that lenders use (Argyle, Plaid) that don't give workers any ownership, or consumer apps that track income but don't produce credit-ready outputs (Steady, Mint).

Krostio bridges both worlds. It's the only platform that:

1. **Scores gig income** — Not just raw data, but a contextual 300-850 score that underwriters can action
2. **Gives workers ownership** — Data is worker-owned and portable via on-chain attestation on Base L2
3. **Works instantly** — No bank statements, no tax returns, no phone calls to employers. Connect OAuth and share a link in 2 minutes
4. **Serves both sides of the market** — Consumer app for workers, API + PDF for lenders, all from one platform

**Key competitors:** Argyle (enterprise data only, no scoring), Nova Credit (cross-border scoring, not gig-native), SteadyIQ (government benefits pivot, no credit product)

**Current stage:** Pre-launch with 2,400 beta users connected and generating scores

**Built on:** Next.js, Supabase, Base L2, Stripe, PostHog, Plaid

Krostio fixes this by turning gig earnings into a verified creditworthiness score (300-850 from Uber, DoorDash, Upwork, and more) that workers can share with any lender, landlord, or leasing agent.

**The longer version:**

I spent 3 years as a gig worker myself. Made good money. Saved diligently. Then tried to get a mortgage and got told "we can't verify your income." Not because my income wasn't real — but because the lending system was designed in 1950 and hasn't updated its definition of "employment."

So we built Krostio.

Connect your gig platforms via OAuth (30 seconds each, we never see your password). We analyze 9 factors across your earnings — volume, consistency, platform diversity, tenure, earnings trajectory, and more — and generate a Krost Score from 300-850.

Then you get a shareable link and PDF report. Send it to a lender, landlord, credit union, or auto dealer. They open it, see verified structured income data, and can make a real decision.

**The four pillars:**

No W-2 needed. No bank statement dump. No pay stub fax machine called an "employer."

We're pre-launch with 2,400 beta users already connected and scoring. Free to start. Check your score at krostio.vercel.app.

Happy to answer any questions — about how the score works, how on-chain attestation helps, which platforms we support, or why I think the 1099 is not a liability but a proof of income.
