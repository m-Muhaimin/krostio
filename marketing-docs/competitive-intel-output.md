## 3. Strategic Whitespace

**Krost's unique intersection:** On-chain income verification + credit scoring that bridges gig-worker multi-platform earnings to **both** traditional lenders AND DeFi protocols.

### 6 Specific Gaps in the Market
1. **No platform** combines bank-deposit income data (Plaid) with on-chain score attestation (Base L2)
2. **No consumer-facing product** gives gig workers a portable, shareable, worker-controlled credit score
3. **Lenders have no unified API** for gig-worker income verification — every integration is custom
4. **DeFi lending lacks real-world income data** — on-chain scores are wallet-activity only
5. **Plaid/Argyle/Truv are data pipes, not credit engines** — they provide raw data, not a decision-ready metric
6. **No multi-platform aggregation** for gig workers using Uber + DoorDash + Instacash simultaneously

## 4. Positioning Statement

**One-liner:** Krost turns gig workers' multi-platform earnings into a verifiable, on-chain credit score that lenders trust and DeFi protocols can integrate — bridging 60M gig workers to fair credit.

## 5. Target ICPs

| ICP | Pain | Desire |
|-----|------|--------|
| **Multi-platform Gig Worker** | Denied for loans/rental because traditional credit scores miss gig income | A portable, shareable credit profile that reflects actual earnings |
| **Online / Alternative Lender** | Can't underwrite gig workers because W2/paystub verification fails | An API that returns a decision-ready income verification score |
| **DeFi Protocol** | No real-world income data for underwriting in smart contracts | On-chain attestation bringing off-chain earnings on-chain |

## 6. Key Differentiators

1. **Dual Score Architecture** — Income Consistency Score (0-100, transparent) + Krost Score (300-850, lender-compatible, 4 tiers)
2. **On-Chain Attestation** — Scores minted as SBTs on Base L2, verifiable by any smart contract
3. **Multi-Platform Aggregation** — Unified view across Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, Instacart, etc. via Plaid
4. **Shareable Report + PDF** — Link-based verification lenders access with email capture
5. **Consumer-Controlled Sharing** — Worker shares their score on their terms, not a bureau pull
